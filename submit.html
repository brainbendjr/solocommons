SOLO SHOW ABOUT ARCHIVE SUBMIT


Solo Show is always open for submissions.
We welcome offsite exhibitions and other experimental works.

We currently have a strong focus on work that:
1. Takes place outside of a gallery setting
2. Is temporary, ephemeral, or viewable only online

We prefer files sent via a Google Drive folder with edit permissions for soloshowproject@gmail.com
Please include any titles, text, links, or other information you'd like to publish.

We prioritize exhibitions which are previously unpublished (though your project may include old work, presented in a new form or setting).
We are always open to curatorial collabs with artists, offspaces and independent initiatives.
Open submissions are published at a moderate pace between our curated shows.

The nature of your solitude is open to your intepretation.


Artists are encouraged to familiarize themselves with our visual language before submitting.
An ongoing invitation to shape our Commons is here.
